Which TV show should young children watch?

Every parent of a young child wonders which TV shows are best for the child to watch. As language development and literacy are one of the biggest predictors of success in school/life, many parents want to know which TV shows are the best for developing language

This page ranks popular children's TV programming available on Amazon Prime and Netflix by their ability to help aid in language development through metrics such as WPM and vocabulary. This page also allows you to explore metrics such as the words spoken (does the show use "cold" or "chilly" to refer to the weather?), the vocabulary development (does the show introduce new words frequently, AND repeat the new words enough times for children to learn through context clues?), and the words per minute (generally, the more words the better for literacy!)
